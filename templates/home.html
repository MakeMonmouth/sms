Who are we?

Open to anyone over the age of 18, Make Monmouth is a space where you can create, design, tinker, and hack on your projects in the company of like-minded people.

Equipment Access

Gain access to tools that you've always wanted to use.

From multimeters and sewing machines to 3D printers and laser cutters we hope to eventually provide all the tools you need to finish that dream project

Meet others

Deep down, all makers are geeks.

We want to provide a shared space in which folks from all walks of life can create in their own style, whilst meeting other people who understand the passion to build thing regardless of the medium used

Build Amazing Things

It doesn't matter what you do

Previous meetings have included everything from furniture painting and restoration works through to Artificial Intelligence and Robots. It doesn't matter what you make, or how you make it, we'll find room for you to express yourself

Where do we meet?

You can find us at the Woodland Community Stores in Wyesham, Monmouth

We meet twice a month, with the first Thursday of the month open to guests and people wishing to become members.
